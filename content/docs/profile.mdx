Every StreamNook member has an identity that shows up in chat and on a profile card: a member number with a rank, the cosmetics you have equipped, and accolades you have earned.

## Member number and rank

When you join, you get a permanent member number based on signup order. Lower numbers are rarer and map to a rank tier:

| Rank | Member number |
| --- | --- |
| Ethereal | 1 |
| Mythic | up to 10 |
| Ascendant | up to 100 |
| Founder | up to 1000 |
| Member | everyone after |

Your rank tints your badge and tier card. Hover a StreamNook badge in chat to reveal the member's number and rank.

## The profile card

Click your badge, or another member's, to open the profile card. It pulls together:

- Member number and rank.
- Equipped cosmetics, shown in a consistent order.
- Twitch info such as account age and followers.
- Lifetime stats like hours watched and messages sent.
- Earned accolades.

## Your public profile

Other members can view your profile. You decide what it shows. In your profile settings you can hide individual sections, for example hours watched, Twitch info, lifetime stats, top emotes, or accolades. A live preview updates as you change things.

## Accolades

Accolades are milestone badges that appear on your profile. They come in a few flavors:

- **Milestones** for activity, such as messages sent, hours watched, streams watched, Drops claimed, points held, cosmetics collected, and Twitch account age.
- **Secret accolades** that stay hidden until you earn them, with playful unlocks tied to special member numbers and combined achievements.
- **Seasonal accolades** that appear when you open StreamNook during a holiday window.
- A **cake day** accolade on your Twitch account anniversary.

Some accolades unlock cosmetics. For example, an accolade can unlock a profile Atmosphere that is then free for anyone who earned it. See [Cosmetics](/docs/cosmetics).
