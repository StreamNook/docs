StreamNook shows emotes from Twitch and the three major third-party providers, with no setup. Animated emotes play smoothly without affecting the stream.

## Supported providers

| Provider | What it covers |
| --- | --- |
| **Twitch** | Global, subscriber, bits, and special-event emotes. |
| **7TV** | Community emote sets, including animated and zero-width emotes. |
| **BTTV** | BetterTTV global and channel emotes. |
| **FFZ** | FrankerFaceZ global and channel emotes. |

All four are matched in chat automatically. When a name matches more than one provider, StreamNook picks one consistently so the same word always shows the same emote.

## Using emotes

- **Type the name** and it renders inline as you send.
- **Hover** an emote in chat to see a larger preview with its name and provider. Set the preview size in **Settings, Chat**.
- **Emote picker:** open it from the chat input to browse and search. Tabs separate Twitch, 7TV, BTTV, FFZ, your favorites, and emote sets.
- **Autocomplete:** start typing an emote name and press `Tab` to cycle through matches.

## Favorites and emote sets

- **Star** any emote in the picker to add it to your Favorites tab for quick access.
- Browse curated and channel **emote sets** from the picker or **Settings, Emote Sets**.

## Twitch subscriber and bits emotes

- **Subscriber emotes** show in the picker and are usable in channels you subscribe to.
- **Bits emotes** (cheermotes) appear when you cheer, for example `Cheer100`, colored by the bits tier.

## Sizing and spacing

Fine-tune how emotes look in chat in **Settings, Chat, Chat Design**:

- **Emote scale** to make emotes larger or smaller.
- **Emote margin** for spacing around them.
- **Hover preview size** for the popup when you mouse over an emote.

## Caching

StreamNook caches emotes and badges to disk so they load instantly and never stutter the video. Caching runs politely in the background and speeds up while the emote picker is open.

Manage the cache in **Settings, Cache**:

- Set how long cached items are kept.
- View cache info or open the cache folder.
- **Clear cache** to free disk space. Items re-download as needed.

<Note>
Chat and emotes never compete with your stream for bandwidth. The video always gets priority, and off-screen messages use almost no memory.
</Note>
