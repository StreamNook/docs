title: Twitch Drops

---

StreamNook tracks your Twitch Drops, earns progress as you watch, and can claim finished Drops for you. Once it is set up, you mostly leave it alone.

## Connect Drops

Drops use a one-time sign-in that is separate from your main Twitch login.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Drops center">
    Click the gift icon in the title bar or press `Ctrl+Shift+D`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in for Drops">
    StreamNook shows a code and a Twitch link. Open the link, enter the code, and approve. This is only required once.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When Drops are connected, the gift icon in the title bar shows live progress while you are earning.

## The Drops center

The Drops center has a few views:

- **Campaigns:** every active Drop campaign, shown as a grid of games. The game you are currently earning in is pinned to the top, followed by your favorites. Search by game or campaign name.
- **Inventory:** Drops you are working on and Drops you have claimed, filterable by status.
- **Stats:** totals such as Drops claimed, points earned, and recent activity.

Each campaign card shows its progress and how many Drops are in progress.

## How earning works

- Progress is earned for the channel you are actively watching, as long as the video is playing. Pausing pauses progress.
- Drop progress is tracked on your account, not per channel, so you keep your progress if you switch to another channel running the same campaign.
- StreamNook refreshes your progress regularly so the numbers stay current.

## Claiming

By default, StreamNook claims Drops for you the moment they finish. You can change this in **Settings** (Drops options):

- **Auto-claim Drops:** claim finished Drops automatically. On by default.
- **Notify when a Drop is ready:** get a heads-up when something is claimable.
- **Notify when a Drop is claimed:** get a confirmation on auto-claim.

If you prefer to claim manually, turn auto-claim off and use the claim button on each Drop in the inventory.

## Favorites

Star a game in the Drops center to keep it near the top of your campaign list. Favorites only affect sorting, so they are a convenience, not a setting that changes what you earn.

<Note>
Some campaigns are limited to specific channels. Those still appear so you can see them, but you earn their Drops only while watching an eligible channel.
</Note>

## Disconnecting

To sign out of Drops, use the Drops logout option. Your main Twitch login is unaffected.
