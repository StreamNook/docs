StreamNook is a desktop app for watching Twitch. It gives you a clean player, multi-stream viewing, a fast chat with full third-party emote support, automatic Drops and channel point collection, and a customizable identity, all in one window without a browser.

This guide covers everything: installing the app, every player control, watching many streams at once, chat and emotes, Drops and channel points, your profile and cosmetics, the full settings reference, keyboard shortcuts, and plugins.
