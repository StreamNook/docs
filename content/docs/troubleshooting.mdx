---
title: Troubleshooting
description: Fixes for common StreamNook problems.
---

Most issues clear up with a stream reload or a restart. If something is stuck, work down this list.

## A stream is buffering or stuttering

- Press `R` to restart the stream, or use the reload button on the player.
- Lower the quality from the gear menu.
- In **Settings, Player**, set **Low Latency Target Mode** to **Balanced** or **Quality**, and raise the **live edge gap** so there is more buffer.
- Open the gear menu's **Stats** to check dropped frames and bandwidth.

## Wrong account perks, or ads when you expect none

If your subscriber or Turbo benefits are not applying, the app may be using a different account's session. Switch to the correct account in **Settings, Profile**, or sign out and back in. Switching accounts resets cached entitlements, which usually fixes it.

## A cosmetic or unlock did not appear

Unlocks normally land within a few seconds of a contribution. If one is missing:

- Make sure you are signed in to the account you contributed on.
- Alt-tab away and back to the app, which prompts it to re-check your entitlements.
- Restart the app.

## Drops are not earning

- Confirm Drops are connected. Open the Drops center (`Ctrl+Shift+D`); if it asks you to sign in, complete the one-time Drops sign-in.
- Progress only counts while the video is playing on an eligible channel. Make sure the stream is not paused.
- Some campaigns only count on specific channels.

See [Drops](/docs/drops).

## Emotes look low quality or are missing

- Open **Settings, Cache** and clear the cache, then reopen the channel so emotes re-download.
- Adjust **emote scale** in **Settings, Chat** if they are too small or large.

## The app looks wrong after a theme change

Reset to a built-in theme in **Settings, Theme**. If glass panels are hard to read, raise or lower **glass transparency** in the same tab.

## Reset without losing everything

Before any larger reset, export your settings from **Settings, Backup** so you can restore them. Your cosmetics, rank, and tenure are tied to your Twitch account, so they return when you sign back in even after a reinstall.

## Still stuck

- Check **Settings, What's New** to confirm you are on the latest version. If an update button is showing at the top of the window, grab the newest build. See [Installation](/docs/installation#updates).
- Export your logs from **Settings, Support** and report the issue.
- Ask in the [community Discord](https://discord.gg/2xvuF9TES7).
