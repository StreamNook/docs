StreamNook is free. If you want to support the project, two optional tiers unlock extra ways to customize your identity. Everything you unlock is permanent.

## The tiers

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Member">
    Free for everyone. Includes your member number and rank, the default badge, and the Tier aura profile theme.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Supporter">
    A one-time contribution. Adds the Supporter badge and the 7TV paint profile theme.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Subscriber">
    A monthly contribution. Adds the Subscriber badge and StreamNook Atmospheres.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to support

In the app, click any locked cosmetic in your profile customization. It opens the support page with your Twitch handle already filled in, so the unlock lands on the right account. You can also go straight to the [support page](https://streamnook.app/support).

After you complete a contribution on the web, your unlock appears in the app within a few seconds. No restart needed.

## Permanent unlocks

Once a tier grants a cosmetic, it is yours to keep. Your Supporter and Subscriber badges and everything they unlock stay available even if a subscription later ends. Re-subscribing continues your month count from where it left off.

<Note>
Your cosmetics and tenure are stored against your Twitch identity, not the app install, so they follow you to any device where you sign in.
</Note>
