The first time you open StreamNook, a setup wizard helps you sign in and set a few preferences. You can change everything later in Settings.

## Sign in with Twitch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start sign-in">
    Click **Sign in** in the app. StreamNook opens Twitch's official login page so you authorize the app directly with Twitch. StreamNook never sees your password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Log in on Twitch and approve access. The window returns to StreamNook automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You are in">
    Your avatar, follows, and entitlements load. Signed-in features such as following, subscribing, chat, Drops, and channel points are now available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Being signed in unlocks:

- Watching with your account, so subscriber and Turbo benefits apply.
- Sending chat, following, and subscribing from inside the app.
- Twitch Drops and channel point collection.
- Your StreamNook profile, rank, and cosmetics.

## Multiple accounts

You can link more than one Twitch account and switch between them.

- **Add an account:** open the profile section at the top of Settings and add another account. Each account signs in through Twitch the same way.
- **Switch accounts:** pick the account you want to make active. The app rebuilds your chat identity, follows, and entitlements for that account.
- **Sign out:** signing out of the active account promotes another linked account if you have one, otherwise it returns you to the signed-out state.

<Tip>
Switching accounts also resets account-specific things like Turbo and subscription status, so an alt account never inherits your main account's perks by mistake.
</Tip>

## Enable Twitch Drops

Drops use a separate Twitch sign-in from your main login. This is a one-time step.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Drops">
    Click the gift icon in the title bar, or press `Ctrl+Shift+D`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the device sign-in">
    StreamNook shows a short code and a Twitch link. Open the link, enter the code, and approve.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    Once approved, StreamNook tracks and claims Drops in the background while you watch. See [Drops](/docs/drops) for the full workflow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The setup wizard

The wizard runs only on first launch and covers:

- **Twitch sign-in**, as above.
- **A few starting preferences** for the player, chat, and theme.
- **An optional plugin step**, in case you want to add an add-on right away.

You can skip any optional step and revisit it later. Once finished, the wizard does not appear again. Everything it sets is editable in [Settings](/docs/settings).
