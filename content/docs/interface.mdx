StreamNook runs in a single window with its own title bar, a sidebar for navigating channels, the player, and chat. This page explains where everything is.

## The title bar

The custom title bar runs across the top. From left to right it holds the StreamNook logo, a row of quick-access buttons, and the window controls.

| Button | What it opens |
| --- | --- |
| **Logo** | The About dialog. |
| **Drops** (gift icon) | The Drops center. Shows live progress while mining, and a "sign in" cue if Drops are not connected yet. |
| **Badges** (medal icon) | Your badges and 7TV paints. |
| **Whispers** (message icon) | Your whispers. |
| **Marketplace** (store icon) | The plugin marketplace. Shows a count badge when plugin updates are available. |
| **Settings** (gear icon) | The settings dialog. |
| **Minimize / Maximize / Close** | Standard window controls. |

You can drag the window by its title bar. Plugins can add their own buttons to this row.

## The sidebar

The sidebar lists your followed channels and recommendations so you can jump between streams. You choose how much of it shows in **Settings, Interface, Sidebar Display Mode**:

- **Expanded:** always full width with names.
- **Compact:** avatars only, expanding on hover. This is the default.
- **Hidden:** appears only when you hover the edge.
- **Disabled:** off entirely.

Click any channel to open it in the player.

## Player and chat layout

When you open a stream, the player fills the main area and chat sits beside it.

- **Resize chat** by dragging the divider between the player and chat.
- **Chat placement** can be on the right or on the bottom.
- **Hide chat** for a clean, full-width player.
- **Lock aspect ratio** (on by default in **Settings, Player**) keeps the video at 16:9 by resizing the window to fit the player plus chat as you change things.

### Theater mode

Press `T` to toggle theater mode. The sidebar and chat hide and the video fills the window. Press `T` again to return. You can define your own theater sizes under **Settings, Interface, Compact View Presets**.

### Fullscreen

Press `F` or use the fullscreen button on the player to go fully fullscreen. See [Player and controls](/docs/player) for all playback keys.

## Themes

StreamNook ships with a set of themes grouped into categories in **Settings, Theme**, including a true-black OLED option. You can:

- Pick any built-in theme.
- Adjust **glass transparency** for the blurred panels.
- Choose an **interface font**.
- Build and save your own custom theme with full control over background, text, accent, and highlight colors.

## Motion and performance

If animations feel heavy, set **Settings, Interface, Motion** to **Reduced** or **Off**. Off gives the snappiest feel on lower-end machines while keeping things like loading spinners working.
